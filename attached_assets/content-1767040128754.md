What types of collabs are available to join or host?

At The Collab Room, every format supports two roles: **Hosts** (looking for partners) and **Guests** (looking for exposure).

Audio & Video

- Twitter Spaces• As Host: Find expert speakers for your panel.• As Guest: Apply to speak on established spaces.
- Podcasts• As Host: Find high-quality guests.• As Guest: Get booked on top Web3 pods.

Co-Marketing

- Campaigns• As Host: Lead a joint giveaway or Quest.• As Guest: Partner with brands for reach.
- NewslettersSwap shoutouts in email blasts to cross-pollinate subscriber bases.

Why a Telegram bot

Telegram is where crypto teams already live. No new login to remember, no platform to learn. Plus, Telegram allows for really easy messaging integration which is super important when organizing a collab.

How do you verify members?

We manually verify every applicant. We check: company website exists, Twitter account is legitimate and has 2,000+ followers, and project shows signs of real activity.

Is this actually free?

Yes, the beta is completely FREE for all qualifying web3 brands/companies! No credit card, no trial that converts to paid. We're testing the model before deciding on pricing.

What if someone spams me?

You control everything. As a host, you approve every request. Plus, we ban spammers immediately.
