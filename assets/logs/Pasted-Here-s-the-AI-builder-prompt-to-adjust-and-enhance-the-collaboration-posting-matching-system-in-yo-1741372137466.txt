AI Builder Prompt: Enhancing Collaboration Matching in Collab Room

Overview

	•	Short Description

Newsletter Feature

Blog Post Feature
	•	Blog Post Topic
	•	Link to Blog
	•	Estimated Release Date

Co-Marketing on Twitter

5️⃣ Matching & Notification System

Once a collaboration is posted, the system will:

	•	“There are X new opportunities that match your preferences. Would you like to review them?”
